#wget
The wget command in Linux is a free, command-line utility used to download files from the internet. It operates non-interactively in the background, meaning it can continue downloading even if you log out or disconnect from your session. It supports HTTP, HTTPS, and FTP protocols, as well as retrieval through HTTP proxies.
